The Brief

As a recent graduate, you decide to put your design skills toward setting up a new social enterprise in the city you live in. You have the opportunity to conceptualize the scope of this business and the product or service this startup offers, using a human-centered design approach.

Areas of inquiry to consider: improved social equity; sustainability; healthy communities; affordability; food security; education; accessibility; arts and culture; civic participation.

Your designed solution must relate to an identified present day, real problem and consider an appropriate target market carefully. Using UX research and methodologies, you will create a brand, an appropriate MVP digital product as well as a media strategy to launch your product or service.
